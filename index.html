Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp of onions
  • 1 tbsp of capsicum
  • 1 tbsp of tomatos
  • 1/8 tbsp of salt
  • 1 Green chili
  • 1 pinch of tumeric
  • 1 tbsp of oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the pan with oil
  2. Pour in the eggs together with the vegetables
  3. Swirl both the pan and the spatula
  4. Allow the eggs to set
  5. Add the tumeric and salt
  6. Roll and slide the omelet onto a plate
  7. Shape the omelete and finish with a pat of butter
  8. Serve while still hot
